What this tool does

Enter your proposed Phase 3 glioblastoma trial — drug, target, patient population, primary endpoint. The tool checks it against 21 real historical GBM trials and flags the failure patterns it most closely matches.

Most cancer drugs that reach Phase 3 fail. Those failures aren't random — they follow patterns the field keeps repeating. This is a pre-mortem: a structured second opinion before $500M and five years go into a trial.

Example output Trials with this profile (anti-VEGF, all-comers, co-primary OS/PFS) have failed 3 of 3 times in this database. AVAglio, RTOG 0825, and EORTC 26101 all hit PFS without OS — same pattern your design repeats.
